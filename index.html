This initiative can be accomplished by empowering IBM Z developers with the most effective DevOps software to understand applications and proactively identify and correct problems as early as possible within the development process. The approach comprises the ‘Shift Left’ concept, with the ultimate benefits of improved time-to-market and improved quality.
